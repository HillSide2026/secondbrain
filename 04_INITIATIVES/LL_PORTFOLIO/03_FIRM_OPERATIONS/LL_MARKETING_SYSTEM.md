# LL Marketing System

## Purpose
Manage firm-wide marketing activities from lead generation through lead nurture.

## Scope
Covers pre-engagement activities prior to formal onboarding.

## What This System Owns
- Lead generation and capture
- Inquiry handling
- Lead nurture workflows
- Lead Conversion
- Lead Onboarding

## What This System Does NOT Own
- Engagement onboarding
- Legal advice or analysis
- Pricing or commercial negotiation
- Matter execution

## Interfaces With Other LL Systems
- LL Sales System
- LL Delivery System
- LL Fulfillment System

## Design Points
- When does a lead transition to sales – after they become a client.
- What qualifies an inquiry for escalation – inscope with budget
