# LL Fulfillment System

## Purpose
Support execution and administration of approved legal work after delivery framing is complete.

## Scope
Covers matter administration and matter-level accounting once work is authorized.

## What This System Owns
- Matter setup and tracking
- Administrative workflows
- Matter-level accounting inputs

## What This System Does NOT Own
- Engagement scoping or delivery decisions
- Legal judgment or analysis
- Firm-wide financial reporting
- Marketing or sales activity

## Interfaces With Other LL Systems
- LL Delivery System
- LL Management System
- Practice Area Systems

## Open Design Questions
- How are matters instantiated from delivery decisions?
- What data flows into accounting vs management?
