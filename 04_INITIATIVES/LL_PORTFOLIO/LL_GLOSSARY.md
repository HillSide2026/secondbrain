# LL Glossary

This glossary defines key terms as they are used within the LL Portfolio.
Definitions are descriptive, not prescriptive, and reflect current system usage.

---

## LL
Refers to the firm as the legal operator. Used to distinguish firm-level systems, responsibilities, and artifacts from client activities and external legal systems.

---

## Client
An external party receiving legal advisory services from LL under a defined engagement. A client relationship exists only after onboarding acceptance.

---

## Legal System
The external system of statutes, regulations, courts, regulators, and professional obligations that govern client conduct and LL's professional role.

---

## Engagement
A bounded legal relationship between LL and a client, defined by scope, role, assumptions, exclusions, and start/end conditions.

## Matter
A Data Object that contains bounded legal relationship between LL and client.

---

## Practice Area
A domain of legal work organized around subject matter expertise.
Current practice areas include:
- Corporate
- Contracts
- Payment Services

Practice areas define **what type of legal work** is performed, not how delivery is managed.

---

## Firm Operations
Firm-wide systems that support delivery, fulfillment, marketing, sales, and management.
Firm Operations systems are not practice-area specific.

---

## LL System
A firm-wide operational or governance system within LL (e.g., Delivery, Fulfillment, Marketing).
LL Systems define **how the firm operates**, not legal outcomes.

---

## LL Delivery System
The firm-wide system responsible for governing the delivery relationship between LL, clients, and the legal system, including matter scope, matter boundaries, escalation logic, and artifact classification.

---

## LL Fulfillment System
The firm-wide system responsible for matter administration and matter-level accounting.

---

## LL Marketing System
The firm-wide system responsible for pre-engagement activities, including lead generation, inquiry handling, lead nurture, lead conversion and onboarding-related transitions.

---

## LL Sales System
A firm-wide system intended to govern client re-qualification and re-conversion.
