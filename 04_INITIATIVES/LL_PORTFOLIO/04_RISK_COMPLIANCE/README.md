# 04_RISK_COMPLIANCE — Defensive Clarity

## Purpose

Defensive clarity and audit readiness.

## Scope

### In Scope

- Risk registers
- Compliance doctrines
- Violation tracking
- Resolution logs

### Out of Scope

- Declaring compliance status
- Closing issues autonomously
- Waiving controls

## ML1 Authority Statement

ML1 is the sole authority for declaring compliance status, closing issues, and waiving controls. ML2 may track and log but must not make compliance determinations.

## Explicit Prohibitions

ML2 must NOT:

- Declare compliance status
- Close issues autonomously
- Waive controls
- Assert that compliance has been achieved
- Make risk acceptance decisions
