# Common Artifacts: Incorporation

Categories of documents typically assembled in this solution space.

---

## Formation Documents

| Artifact | OBCA | CBCA |
|----------|------|------|
| Articles of Incorporation | OBCA Form 1 | CBCA Form 1 |
| Filing cover sheet | Ontario-specific | Corporations Canada |
| Name clearance (NUANS) | If named | If named |

---

## Organizational Documents

| Artifact | OBCA | CBCA |
|----------|------|------|
| Organizational resolutions (directors) | OBCA-compliant | CBCA-compliant |
| Organizational resolutions (shareholders) | OBCA-compliant | CBCA-compliant |
| By-law No. 1 | Standard form | Standard form |
| Banking resolution | If immediate | If immediate |

---

## Share Documentation

- Share subscription forms
- Share certificates (or uncertificated notice)
- Initial share issuance resolution

---

## Corporate Records

- Register of shareholders
- Register of directors
- Register of officers
- Minute book index
- Minute book shell / organization

---

## Filing Confirmations

| Artifact | OBCA | CBCA |
|----------|------|------|
| Certificate of Incorporation | Ontario | Corporations Canada |
| Certified Articles | If requested | If requested |

---

## Artifact Discipline

- Artifacts are reused across matters and must not be reinvented
- Use statute-appropriate variants only
- Presence of an artifact category does not imply necessity in every matter
- Absence does not imply defect
