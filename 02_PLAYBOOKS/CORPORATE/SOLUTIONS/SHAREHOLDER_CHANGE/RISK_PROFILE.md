# Risk Profile: Shareholder Change

Frequent risk surfaces in this solution space.

---

## Governance Risks

- Control shifts without corresponding governance alignment
- Board composition not reflecting new ownership reality
- Decision thresholds becoming unworkable post-change
- Deadlock risk introduced by new ownership structure

---

## Economic Risks

- Exit rights misaligned with economic participation
- Preference stacking creating unexpected waterfall outcomes
- Anti-dilution mechanisms triggered unexpectedly
- Distribution rights unclear after restructuring

---

## Regulatory Risks

- Regulatory filings lagging transaction reality
- Securities law exemption requirements not satisfied
- Foreign ownership restrictions triggered
- Tax elections missed or filed incorrectly

---

## Contractual Risks

- Existing shareholder agreement restrictions violated
- Pre-emptive rights not properly addressed
- Consent requirements overlooked
- Change of control provisions in other contracts triggered

---

## Process Risks

- Authorization chain incomplete
- Director conflicts not properly managed
- Minority shareholder notice requirements missed
- Documentation not reflecting actual agreement
