# Common Artifacts: Shareholder Agreement

Categories of documents typically assembled in this solution space.

---

## Agreement Documents

| Artifact | SA | USA |
|----------|----|----|
| Base agreement template | SA template | USA template |
| Recitals | Standard | Statutory compliance recitals |
| Definitions | Standard | Standard + statutory terms |

---

## Governance Provisions

| Artifact | SA | USA |
|----------|----|----|
| Board composition | Standard | May be modified/eliminated |
| Reserved matters | Contractual | Statutory effect |
| Decision thresholds | Contractual | Binding on corporation |
| Director power transfer schedule | N/A | Required |

---

## Transfer and Exit Provisions

| Artifact | SA | USA |
|----------|----|----|
| Transfer restrictions | Contractual | Statutory effect |
| Right of first refusal | Standard module | Standard module |
| Tag-along / drag-along | Standard module | Standard module |
| Buy-sell provisions | Optional | Optional |
| Shotgun / put-call | Optional | Optional |

---

## Schedules and Exhibits

- Shareholder schedule (names, shares, percentages)
- Share class summary
- Governance matrix (who approves what)
- Director power transfer schedule (USA only)
- Valuation methodology (if buy-sell included)

---

## Execution Documents

- Signature pages
- Counterpart provisions
- Spousal consent (if required)
- Corporate acknowledgement

---

## USA-Specific Requirements

| Artifact | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Statutory acknowledgement | OBCA s.108 / CBCA s.146 compliance |
| Liability assumption clause | Shareholders accept director duties |
| Certificate notation form | Required notation on share certificates |
| Articles cross-reference | Alignment with constating documents |

---

## Artifact Discipline

- Artifacts are reused across matters and must not be reinvented
- SA and USA templates are distinct; do not merge
- USA artifacts have statutory compliance requirements
- Presence of an artifact category does not imply necessity
- Absence does not imply defect
