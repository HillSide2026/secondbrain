## Governance Structure for the Wiki

### Every doctrinal page should include:
- Definition
- Elements
- Leading Supreme Court authorities
- Divergences across provinces (if material)
- Practical drafting implications

### Separate doctrinal content from:
- Drafting guidance
- Litigation strategy commentary
- Public policy critique

---

## Design Recommendation

### If the audience is senior counsel, avoid:
- Over‑simplification
- Law‑student style summaries
- Blog tone

### Instead, adopt:
- Neutral doctrinal summaries
- Citations to SCC cases
- Cross‑references
- Clear articulation of governing tests
